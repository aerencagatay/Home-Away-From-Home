About the Exhibition

Milano: Home Away From Home explores what it means to build a life in a city that is not your own. Through the works of emerging artists who have experienced migration, displacement, or simply the quiet act of starting over, the exhibition transforms a WWII-era underground shelter into a space of reflection on belonging, identity, and the invisible ties that connect us to the places we call home — even temporarily. Set beneath Piazza Grandi in Milan, the venue itself carries the memory of shelter and refuge, echoing the journeys of the artists within.
